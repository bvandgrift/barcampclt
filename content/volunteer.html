We Need You

Volunteers have the opportunity to:

BarCamp Charlotte runs on the energy of its volunteers and sponsors. Without both, BarCamp Charlotte as you know it wouldn't happen (well..it might, but not nearly as fun). Help us out and volunteer.

  • Learn how to plan, manage, and coordinate an event
  • Network with the brainstormers and innovators of Charlotte
  • Gain school credit for working for an event
  • Have a fun time making a BarCamp happen!

Day Before

Venue Prep

We need a group of people to help us prepare Area 15 for BarCamp the day before the event. Things like setting up chairs and tables, hanging signs, etc.

Tech Prep

We need another group of people to help us setup the BarCamp network, including wireless modems, routers, power, video cameras, microphones, projectors, monitors, etc.

Day Of

Room Coordinators

Help needed during session switches to make sure everyone knows where they're going, and during sessions to make sure each room and presenter has the materials/tools they need. It would be ideal to get enough people helping here so everyone can switch out during the day so each person only has to commit to 1 or 2 sessions.

Food Support

We need a few people to help make sure the food arrives and is setup for hungry BarCampers

Tech Support

We need a handful of people the day of that can be on call if tech complications arise -- i.e., projectors not working, wifi networks going down, etc. Leave your Twitter name or cell phone numbers so we can summon during the event if needed.
